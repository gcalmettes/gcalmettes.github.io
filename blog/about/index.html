About


Photo from Luis Escobar

I started trail running after I signed up on a whim to the 2012 TNFC 50K. I loved every aspects of it, the adrenaline of racing, running beautiful landscapes, and meeting amazing individuals. Since then, I have been hanging out in the mountains as often as possible, being grateful to have the beautiful Santa Monica Mountains in my backyard ;)

When I am not running, I like reading, swimming, spending time with friends, and learning on the internet.
